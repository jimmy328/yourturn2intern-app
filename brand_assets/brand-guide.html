Voice & Tone
  • Civic, not political. We're non-partisan — equal access for students regardless of their political views.
  • Student-first. Speak directly to young people. Keep it approachable, energetic, and real.
  • Local pride. Long Island isn't a consolation prize. Lean into community and proximity.
  • Low-pressure. No hard sells, no jargon. This platform is genuinely free and easy — say so.
Do's & Don'ts
Do
  • Use "YourTurn2Intern" as one word
  • Keep "2" blue in the logo
  • Use Nunito 900 for hero headlines
  • Lead with "free" and "local"
  • Maintain contrast on all backgrounds
Don't
  • Write "Your Turn 2 Intern" (no spaces)
  • Recolor or modify the "2"
  • Use coral for body text
  • Mix fonts outside the type system
  • Use the logo on busy backgrounds
