## EMAIL 1: Campaign Outreach
**To:** Campaign manager / candidate contact
**Subject:** Free way to find student interns for your campaign (Long Island)

---

Hi [Name],

Congrats on the campaign for [Office]. Looks like a real race.

I'm Jeremy, founder of **YourTurn2Intern**, a free platform where Long Island students can find civic internships. We connect students who want hands-on experience in local politics with campaigns, government offices, and nonprofits. We'd love to list yours.

Most campaigns we talk to are already planning to bring on a student or two. This just makes sure the right people can find you.

If you want, I can send over the form, or we can jump on a quick call.

Thanks for your time, and good luck this cycle.
