YourTurn2Intern connects Nassau and Suffolk County students with internships at local campaigns, government offices, and nonprofits — for free.
What is YourTurn2Intern?
A free platform built for Long Island students

YourTurn2Intern is a searchable database of civic internships and volunteer opportunities across Nassau and Suffolk County. Students can browse by location, type, and format — and apply directly to campaigns, town offices, county agencies, and nonprofits.

Every listing is local, vetted, and free to access. There's no cost to students, and no sign-up required to browse.
